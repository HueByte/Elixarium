# Umbral Draught

Fermented in absolute darkness from the ink of deep-sea cephalopods and shadow-caught cobwebs. This inky draught grants the drinker *passage through darkness* itself — shadows become doorways, and the night a navigable sea.

## Preparation

1. In a chamber sealed against all light, place a vessel of obsidian
2. Pour in the ink of a deep-sea cephalopod, harvested at the new moon
3. Weave three shadow-caught cobwebs into the ink using bone needles
4. Grind obsidian dust with a pestle of blackthorn and sift it into the mixture
5. Seal the vessel and leave it undisturbed in total darkness for twenty-one days
6. On the twenty-second night, open the vessel — if done correctly, the liquid will absorb all nearby light
7. Decant swiftly into a lightproof flask; any exposure to sunlight will render it inert

---

- **Potency:** Formidable
- **Duration:** Until first light
- **Catalyst:** Obsidian dust
- **Caution:** Never drink at noon
