# Umbral Draught

Fermented in absolute darkness from the ink of deep-sea cephalopods and shadow-caught cobwebs. This inky draught grants the drinker *passage through darkness* itself — shadows become doorways, and the night a navigable sea.

---

- **Potency:** Formidable
- **Duration:** Until first light
- **Catalyst:** Obsidian dust
- **Caution:** Never drink at noon
