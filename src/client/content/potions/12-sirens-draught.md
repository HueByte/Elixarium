# Siren's Draught

A rich, opaque essence of white rum bound with coconut cream and the concentrated nectar of pineapple. When blended with ice, it yields a *draught of tropical oblivion* — sweet, creamy, and perilously easy to consume.

## Essence — 50ml

- 25ml white rum
- 15ml coconut cream
- 10ml pineapple juice concentrate

## Preparation

1. Warm the coconut cream gently until it flows freely — do not boil
2. Combine the white rum and pineapple concentrate in the vessel and stir
3. Add the coconut cream in a slow stream, stirring constantly to prevent separation
4. Seal and chill — shake the vessel before each use, as the cream may settle
5. The essence holds for up to a week, though fresher is always finer

## To Awaken

Pour the essence into a blender with a cup of crushed ice and **100ml pineapple juice**. Blend until smooth and creamy. Pour into a rounded glass and garnish with a wedge of pineapple. For a lighter draught, simply shake with ice and top with **150ml pineapple juice**.

---

- **Base spirit:** White rum
- **Character:** Creamy, tropical, sweet
- **Best diluted with:** Pineapple juice and ice
- **Kinship:** Piña Colada
