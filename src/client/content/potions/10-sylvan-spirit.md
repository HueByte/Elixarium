# Sylvan Spirit

An aromatic essence of white rum steeped in the verdant breath of mint and sharpened with lime. When awakened with soda, it unfolds into a *draught of green reverie* — cool, herbaceous, and endlessly refreshing.

## Essence — 50ml

- 30ml white rum
- 10ml fresh lime juice
- 10ml mint simple syrup

## Preparation

1. Brew the mint syrup: steep a generous handful of fresh mint leaves in hot simple syrup for ten minutes, then strain and cool
2. Combine the white rum and mint syrup in the vessel
3. Add the fresh lime juice and swirl gently to unite the elements
4. Seal and store cold — the essence keeps well for a week, though the mint fades slowly

## To Awaken

Pour the essence into a tall glass over crushed ice. Add **5–6 fresh mint leaves**, pressing them gently against the glass. Top with **150ml soda water** and stir once, slowly. Crown with a sprig of mint and a lime wheel.

---

- **Base spirit:** White rum
- **Character:** Cool, herbaceous, lively
- **Best diluted with:** Soda water and fresh mint
- **Kinship:** Mojito
