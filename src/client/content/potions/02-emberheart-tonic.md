# Emberheart Tonic

Brewed in a crucible of volcanic glass, this amber tonic pulses with inner warmth. A single draught kindles the vital fire within, granting *extraordinary resilience* against bitter cold and the creeping numbness of despair.

## Preparation

1. Heat a crucible of volcanic glass over dragonbreath coals until it glows dull red
2. Place three salamander scales at the bottom and allow them to curl and blacken
3. Pour in a measure of aged brandy infused with cinnamon bark and clove
4. As the mixture simmers, add ground capsicum root one pinch at a time, never more than seven
5. Stir clockwise with an iron rod until the liquid turns from amber to deep crimson
6. Remove from heat and let cool naturally — never quench with water
7. Decant into a clay vessel and seal with beeswax

---

- **Potency:** Moderate
- **Duration:** Six hours, steady
- **Catalyst:** Salamander scale
- **Caution:** Inflammable near open flame
