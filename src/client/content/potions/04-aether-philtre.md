# Æther Philtre

A vaporous elixir captured in a crystal phial, shimmering between states of matter. When inhaled, the drinker experiences a *lightening of the corpus* — gravity loosens its hold and one may drift upon currents of thought.

## Preparation

1. Procure a crystal phial with a hermetic seal — no ordinary glass will contain the vapour
2. At midnight, expose a dish of quicksilver to open starlight for one full hour
3. Collect the faint condensation that forms on the quicksilver's surface using a feather brush
4. Transfer the dew into the phial and add three grains of stardust residue
5. Seal immediately and shake in a figure-eight pattern forty times
6. Store inverted in a dark cabinet for a fortnight, turning the phial daily
7. The philtre is ready when the contents shift between liquid and gas of their own accord

- **Potency:** Extreme
- **Duration:** One hour, volatile
- **Catalyst:** Stardust residue
- **Caution:** Tether yourself firmly
