# Frostbite Elixir

A bracing essence of crystalline spirits tempered with the sting of ginger root and the sharp kiss of lime. When awakened with ginger beer, it becomes a *draught of Northern vigour* — warming the belly while chilling the tongue.

## Essence — 50ml

- 30ml vodka
- 10ml fresh lime juice
- 10ml ginger syrup

## Preparation

1. Combine the vodka and ginger syrup in your vessel and stir until unified
2. Press the lime through cloth to extract clear juice, then add to the mixture
3. Seal the vessel tightly — the essence keeps its potency for up to a fortnight when chilled

## To Awaken

Pour the essence over ice in a copper mug or tall glass. Crown with **150ml ginger beer** and stir gently. Garnish with a wheel of lime and a sliver of crystallised ginger.

---

- **Base spirit:** Vodka
- **Character:** Sharp, fiery, refreshing
- **Best diluted with:** Ginger beer
