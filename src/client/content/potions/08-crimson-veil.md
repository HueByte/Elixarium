# Crimson Veil

A jewel-toned concentrate of clear spirits veiled in the blush of cranberry and the warmth of orange liqueur. When shaken and strained, it yields a *draught of subtle sophistication* — tart, citric, and dangerously smooth.

## Essence — 50ml

- 25ml vodka
- 10ml Cointreau or triple sec
- 10ml cranberry juice, unsweetened
- 5ml fresh lime juice

## Preparation

1. Pour the vodka and orange liqueur into the vessel first, allowing them to marry
2. Add the cranberry juice — seek the unsweetened variety for a truer colour and sharper edge
3. Finish with the lime juice, freshly pressed
4. Seal and store chilled — the essence holds for up to ten days

## To Awaken

Shake the essence vigorously with ice until the vessel frosts. Strain into a chilled coupe glass. Optionally, top with **50ml sparkling water** for a longer drink. Adorn with a twist of orange peel, expressed over the surface.

---

- **Base spirit:** Vodka
- **Character:** Tart, elegant, citric
- **Best diluted with:** Ice and a shake, or sparkling water
- **Kinship:** Cosmopolitan
