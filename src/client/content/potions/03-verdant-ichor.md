# Verdant Ichor

Pressed from the heartwood of an ancient yew and mingled with morning dew collected at the vernal equinox. This verdant liquid accelerates the body's *natural restoration*, knitting wounds and mending fractures at remarkable speed.

## Preparation

1. At the vernal equinox, bore into the heartwood of a yew no younger than three hundred years
2. Collect the slow amber sap in a glass vial — no more than a thimbleful
3. Before sunrise, gather morning dew from clover using a silver spoon
4. Combine sap and dew in equal measure within a vessel of living wood
5. Add one dryad's tear, allowing it to fall naturally into the mixture
6. Seal and suspend the vessel from an oak branch for seven days and seven nights
7. The ichor is ready when it turns from pale gold to deep emerald green

---

- **Potency:** Strong
- **Duration:** Until next dawn
- **Catalyst:** Dryad's tear
- **Caution:** May attract woodland spirits
