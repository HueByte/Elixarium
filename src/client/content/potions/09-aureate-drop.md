# Aureate Drop

A radiant distillation of lemon and spirit, balanced upon a thread of sugar. When diluted, it becomes a *draught of liquid sunlight* — bright, clean, and effortlessly uplifting. The simplest of the essences, and among the finest.

## Essence — 50ml

- 30ml vodka
- 12ml fresh lemon juice
- 8ml rich simple syrup (2:1 sugar to water)

## Preparation

1. Prepare the rich syrup by dissolving two parts sugar in one part hot water, then cool completely
2. Combine the vodka and syrup in the vessel and stir to incorporate
3. Add the lemon juice last — it must be freshly pressed, never bottled
4. Seal and chill — the essence remains vital for up to a week

## To Awaken

Shake the essence hard with ice for ten heartbeats. Strain into a chilled glass with a sugar-rimmed edge. For a longer draught, top with **120ml sparkling water** or chilled lemonade.

---

- **Base spirit:** Vodka
- **Character:** Bright, tart, clean
- **Best diluted with:** Sparkling water or lemonade
- **Kinship:** Lemon Drop
