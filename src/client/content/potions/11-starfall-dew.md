# Starfall Dew

The purest of the rum essences — nothing but spirit, citrus, and a whisper of sweetness. When shaken cold, it becomes a *draught of crystalline clarity* — sharp, balanced, and deceptively potent. A testament to the virtue of restraint.

## Essence — 50ml

- 35ml white rum
- 10ml fresh lime juice
- 5ml rich simple syrup (2:1 sugar to water)

## Preparation

1. Select a white rum of clean character — aged varieties will cloud the essence's purity
2. Prepare the rich syrup and allow it to cool fully before use
3. Combine all three elements in the vessel and stir until the syrup dissolves completely
4. Seal and chill — this essence is best consumed within five days, while the lime is still vivid

## To Awaken

Shake the essence with a generous handful of ice until bitterly cold. Strain into a chilled coupe glass. For a longer drink, pour over ice and add **100ml sparkling water**. A single lime wheel is the only ornament this draught requires.

---

- **Base spirit:** White rum
- **Character:** Clean, sharp, balanced
- **Best diluted with:** Ice alone, or sparkling water
- **Kinship:** Daiquiri
