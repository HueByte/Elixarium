# Quicksilver Essence

A mercurial compound stabilised through seventeen stages of alchemical refinement. This ever-shifting liquid bestows *preternatural reflexes* upon the consumer — time itself appears to thicken, granting moments that stretch like hours.

## Preparation

1. Begin with a base of thrice-purified mercury in an alembic of copper and tin
2. Add a single hummingbird feather, still warm, to the mercury
3. Heat to precisely the temperature at which tin melts — no hotter, no cooler
4. As vapour rises, capture it in a condenser of spiralling glass and return it to the flask
5. Repeat this distillation cycle exactly seventeen times over seventeen hours
6. With each cycle, add one drop of distilled lightning-water from a storm flask
7. The essence is complete when the liquid moves *before* the flask is tilted

---

- **Potency:** Severe
- **Duration:** Thirty minutes, exact
- **Catalyst:** Hummingbird feather
- **Caution:** Do not combine with stimulants
