# Moonveil Elixir

Distilled from nightbloom petals gathered under a waning crescent, this luminous draught bestows upon the imbiber a *sight beyond sight*. The world reveals its hidden geometries — ley lines shimmer like silver threads and the true names of things become briefly legible.

## Preparation

1. Gather seven nightbloom petals under a waning crescent moon, handling them only with silver tongs
2. Crush the petals in a marble mortar with three pinches of powdered moonstone
3. Dissolve the paste in a flask of twice-distilled spring water, stirring *widdershins* exactly thirteen times
4. Seal the flask and bury it beneath an elder tree for three nights
5. On the fourth dawn, unearth the flask and strain through spider-silk cloth
6. Add a single drop of mercury and heat gently until the liquid glows faintly blue
