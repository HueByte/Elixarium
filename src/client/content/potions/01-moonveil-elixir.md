# Moonveil Elixir

Distilled from nightbloom petals gathered under a waning crescent, this luminous draught bestows upon the imbiber a *sight beyond sight*. The world reveals its hidden geometries — ley lines shimmer like silver threads and the true names of things become briefly legible.

---

- **Potency:** Considerable
- **Duration:** Three hours, waning
- **Catalyst:** Powdered moonstone
- **Caution:** Avoid during solar eclipse
